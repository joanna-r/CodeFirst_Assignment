How it works
Follow these simple steps and get ready to go Halfzees!


1. Log-in or Sign-up on your computer, tablet or mobile device
2. Search through available deals listed on our 'Deals' page, selecting which one you would like
3. Click the 'Search' button and we will find another person wanting to share this deal, a message will be sent from Halfzees and if accepted, you will be put in contact with eachother.
4. Have a chat, arrange to meet up and take full advantage of 2 for 1 deals!
